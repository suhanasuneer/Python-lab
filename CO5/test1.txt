ICET has been one of the pioneering self financing colleges in Kerala. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological.
Initially affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, ICET transitioned a new academic chapter in 2015 by affiliating with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU),
thereby aligning itself with a forward-looking and industry-relevant curriculum.
